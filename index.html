TitanHacks

April 11-12, 2020

Sign up! Notice on Coronavirus

TitanHacks is a hackathon hosted online. High school students from LA and beyond will come for the weekend and team up to build an app, game, or website. Workshops and raffles mean fun for everyone from the beginning programmer to the seasoned hackathon veteran. After a day of hard work, judges will pick the best projects and give over $8000 in prizes. Come to meet new friends, create something new, and have fun!

Join the Discord channel for frequent updates! Join the Devpost for more information!

FAQs

How much is TitanHacks?

Nothing! Registration and attendance are 100% free with prizes & workshops included. We pay for shipping prices if you win!

Who can participate?

Any high school student or equivalent can apply. If you're older and interested in mentoring or helping out, email us!

Isn't hacking dangerous?

We're using "hack" in the "life hack" base. "Hack" in this sense means coding apps, games, websites, or any other project idea you might have.

What can I make?

Anything! A website, app, game, hardware hack, anything you can think of. Judges will rate projects based on utility, skill, and community impact.

Is there a code of conduct?

We adhere to the Hack Club Code of Conduct in order to ensure a positive environment for attendees of all backgrounds.

What if I’m new to coding?

At TitanHacks, we welcome students of all levels! In addition, we will have workshops to get you started and mentors to help troubleshoot.

Do I need a team?

Nope! We have a discord channel for those looking for a partner. Any team of 1-4 members is acceptable.

What should I have?

Nothing since it's online! If you are making a hardware hack, please make sure you have the tools you need as we cannot provide.

What are the themes?

There will be four verticals in total. One of them is hardware. Inorder to prevent cheating, the other three will be announced during the opening ceremony.

Where is the opening/ending ceremony and workshops held?

The opening/ending ceremony will be held on a streaming platform (TBD) and workshops on Zoom. Details can be found in our Discord during the event.
